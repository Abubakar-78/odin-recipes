Pasta

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish made from durum wheat flour and water, shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli. Itâ€™s boiled until tender and served with sauces ranging from simple tomato or pesto to rich, creamy varieties, making it a versatile and comforting meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until tender.
  3. Drain the paste but save a little pasta water
  4. Heat your sauce in a pan
  5. Add the pasta to the sauce and mix, using pasta water if needed.
